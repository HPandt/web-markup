The Japanese Garden

Every wanted to get away far away, but dont want to leave the city, then here is the perfect gem for you. Come down to Landgoed Clingendael, and be enchanted by this wonderful park, that’s like no other. Here at the japanese Garden, you’ll be able to experience a small pice of japan, without going far.

Breathtaking view

The Japanese Garden has a unique and special atmosphere. This is due in part to the beautiful moss cover. There are also beautiful and rare plants and trees in the garden. In the spring you can admire the azaleas, rhododendrons and Japanese cherry blossom trees. During the autumn the garden is exceptionally colourful with the autumn hues of the different Japanese maple trees. Do you wish to come and experience it yourself. well follow us and see when its open!

Experience japan's beauty

The garden contains stone lanterns in all shapes and sizes. It also has 2 water casks. One water cask has 4 images of Buddhas. The other water cask is in the shape of a lotus flower. The pavilion offers a great vantage point to admire the garden. It was made by a countess, who travlled to japan and fell in love, she wanted a small piece of the experience with her, so she decided to buuld this lovely garden for all to see and enjoy.
