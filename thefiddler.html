The fiddler

A night out, is something everyone does. Some once in a blue moon, others every so often but it gets boring going to the same popular places that everyone knows. Either cause its filled to the brim or because you’ve been there so much it has gotten stale. Well here’s a fresh new place for you to enjoy a hidden gem just waiting for some one to come by and have the time of their lifes

Have your fill!

The fiddler is a wonderful authentic english pub, that serves authenic beer and alcohol, besides drinks they alse serve a variety of food and snacks ranging from bar snacks (bitterballen, croquets etc) to full course meals like burgers, steaks and their specials of the day. we encourage you to come down and try some of the specialties , it is situated not far from the Haagsche Bluf by Riviervismarkt 1. Not far from the Centrum, this is a perfect place to go before heading out clubbing or to enjoy a fun night. So are you ready to party, then lets check it out

More than food!

But a night out can’t be completed with just food and drinks to liven up the place they also host many fun events you can take part in, from sports events with many discounted drinks and snack to help make watching the game all the more lively and fun to team events en indivdual ones like quiz nights. so thers always something to do while you are out. So come by and enjoy this nice authenic bar and make your night something to remember.
