Stadstuin Emma's hof

have you ever looked for somewhere nice to go early in the morning, maybe for a morning stroll or to just sit and be lost in your own little world. Well heres another gem for you to find in The hague, truly recommended for bright sunny days or warm weather. this lovely park is a breath of fresh air for all to enjoy. It is open at different time throughtout the seasons so check before going

Peace& tranquility

This city garden is not only a good place for a lovely morning stroll but also if you want to just zen out and enjoy nature. You’ll be able to witness their stunning beauty and unique architechure. Besides the gorgeous view and zen atmosphere there is also many events that take place here, that you can enjoy, and so many memories to gain and share. If you wish to see when its open or what’s currently happen take a look here!

Productive time!

The Lovely Emma’s hof(court) isn’t only a lovely place to go and enjoy, but if you’re looking for away to have a produtive morning then come on down, as they accept volunteers to help maintain the garden or help with the events. Perfect place for those with active skills and want to help make a difference. Located down the road from the Theater De Regentes at Regentessekwatier, it’s a most see for all locals. You can’t miss it!
